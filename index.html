LOVE STORY

LIKE Rachel and Ross or Rose and Jack there is Dominic and Rachel. This story, like many great love stories before it, begins in a night class at San Francisco State University where a friendship blossomed amidst the lectures of Journalism 200. And after weeks of obvious glances and gentle flirting Dominic was finally able to convince Rachel to ditch her friend Kim for a night. The two bonded instantly and quickly became inseparable as they connected over their love of the Oakland A's, Tupac and their hatred of Ketchup.

But young love does not come easy and after discovering its trials and tribulations, the two parted ways. It would not be for long, as Dominic discovered life without Rachel was no fun at all and the prospect of living without her was not something he could bear. He made getting her back his top priority and after a second round of courting he successfully won her over again. With a new discovered love the two embarked on an adventure that would take them to The Big Apple, a tour of the eastern seaboard, and eventually back west.

With nearly seven years under their belts and on the brink of a new chapter in their lives, Dominic decided it was time to take the next step. First, he found a ring that matched the elegance, beauty and uniqueness that is Rachel David. A classic looking ring from the early part of last century, that blended white and yellow gold and carried a beautiful (blood free) diamond. With the ring in hand he decided he'd have to propose somewhere that would clearly define how much he cared for her, a place as breathtaking as her, the Grand Canyon.

It took plenty of sleepless nights for Dominic, a few thousand miles, and several weeks for the couple to reach reach the rim of the Canyon. But when they did, it was exactly what the Grand Canyon was supposed to be, the canyons were vast as the couples love for each other. Dominic proposed on March 9th, 2011 in the freezing temperatures of the South Canyon Rim. Rachel said yes and the two lived happily ever after.
